Decsription

This Pistachio Cake boasts a soft, moist crumb with a pronounced pistachio flavor, achieved by using nearly double the amount of pistachios compared to typical recipes. Toasting the pistachios before grinding enhances their nutty essence, ensuring each bite is rich and flavorful. Topped with a light and tangy Cream Cheese Whip, this cake offers a perfect balance of earthiness and subtle sweetness, making it an elegant choice for any occasion.
