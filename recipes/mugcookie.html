Cookie in a Mug

Home

Description

Cookie in a Mug is the ultimate quick treat — a single-serving dessert made in just minutes using your microwave. Perfect for late-night cravings or when you want something sweet without baking a whole batch, these soft and gooey cookies come together in one mug with simple pantry ingredients. Whether you love classic chocolate chip or want to get creative with mix-ins, mug cookies offer delicious comfort in every bite with minimal cleanup.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 4 teaspoons packed light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Place 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter in an 8-ounce microwave-safe mug. Microwave for about 30 seconds until mostly melted.
  2. Add 4 teaspoons of packed light brown sugar, 1 tablespoon of granulated sugar, ¼ teaspoon of vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt to the melted butter. Stir with a spoon until well combined.
  3. Add 1 egg yolk to the mixture and stir until smooth.
  4. Stir in 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour and most of the 2 tablespoons of semisweet chocolate chips until combined. Sprinkle the remaining chocolate chips on top.
  5. Microwave on high for 45 seconds to 1 minute, or until the dough has puffed and appears dry on top.
  6. Allow the cookie to cool for a few minutes before serving.
